The science

Liquid gets there first.

A standard tablet has to survive your digestive tract before any nutrient is released — and as digestion slows with age and modern diets, much of it never makes it. Liquid nutrients arrive pre-digested, crossing into the bloodstream in around 20 minutes.

That's why we press, bottle and ship in glass: maximum bioavailability, zero plastic leaching, fruit traceable to the grower.
